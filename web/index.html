Why Focus Monitor?

Focus Monitor is a monitoring system adapted to visualise different data into the same screen. By doing this, Focus Monitor simplifies the job of the system administrators by showing them that their Data Centres (DCs) are functioning properly, and in case of error, Focus Monitor will help them to identify where and when the error was produced. All of it within the same screen where they can see the different graphs related to their different nodes.

Focus Monitor is focused on merging information taken from Ganglia (monitorisation of data graphs) and Nagios (alert system). Up to this moment, a system administrator had to work with these two programmes at the same time. Despite the great amount of information that helps the administrator to be more precise, these two programmes seem not to be as intuitive as they should for a common and daily use. Focus Monitor centralises these two programmes for an easier and more efficient approach to the problems as well as for taking quicker glimpses of information, with the same precision the other two provide.

By choosing Focus Monitor, you are choosing precision. By choosing Focus Monitor, you are choosing easiness. By choosing Focus Monitor, you are choosing efficiency.
